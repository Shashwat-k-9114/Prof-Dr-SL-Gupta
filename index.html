About

A Scholar Who Builds Institutions

Born 1965. Based in Delhi/NCR.

Dual doctorate holder — Ph.D. and D.Litt. — with a career spanning premier institutions in India and the Sultanate of Oman.

Awarded Best Director — Technical Institutes, 2013 by the National Human Resource Development Network, Patna.

Prof. (Dr.) S.L. Gupta is a distinguished academic leader whose career spans over three decades at the forefront of management education, research, and institutional governance. His trajectory — from lecturer to Director, and from India to the Sultanate of Oman — reflects not merely professional advancement, but a sustained commitment to the highest ideals of academic leadership.

As Professor at the Birla Institute of Technology (Deemed University), Noida, and former Dean of Waljat College of Applied Sciences in Muscat, he has built a rare cross-cultural fluency in the governance frameworks of both Indian and Gulf education systems. This duality has positioned him as an educator and administrator of genuine global standing.

His scholarly output — 32 research papers in peer-reviewed journals, 16 books across major publishers including Tata McGraw-Hill, CRC Press Taylor & Francis, and Excel Books, and 9 doctoral scholars guided to completion — constitutes a body of work that has shaped the discourse in marketing, retail management, banking, and research methodology across India and beyond.

A strategic architect of institutional systems, he has led accreditation processes, designed curricular frameworks from the ground up, directed multi-institutional research projects, and fostered academic partnerships across national boundaries. His leadership philosophy is grounded in the conviction that great institutions are built not through administration alone, but through a living culture of inquiry, inclusion, and intellectual courage.
